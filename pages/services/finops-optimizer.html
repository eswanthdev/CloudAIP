What's Included

6 Optimization Levers

We pull every lever available to reduce your cloud spend.

📏

Right-Sizing Analysis

Identify over-provisioned EC2, RDS, Azure VMs, and GCP instances. Get specific resize recommendations with risk assessment for each change. We analyze CPU, memory, network, and disk utilization patterns to find the optimal instance size.

💳

Reserved Instance & Savings Plan Strategy

Analyze workload patterns and recommend optimal commitment strategy. We manage RI purchases and exchanges, balancing savings against flexibility. Coverage analysis ensures you're not over-committed or under-committed.

🧹

Idle Resource Cleanup

Find and eliminate orphaned EBS volumes, unattached Elastic IPs, idle load balancers, unused snapshots, and zombie resources. These forgotten resources silently drain your budget every month without providing any value.

📦

Storage Optimization

S3 lifecycle policies, EBS volume type optimization, cross-region transfer analysis, and data tiering recommendations. We ensure your data is stored on the most cost-effective storage tier based on actual access patterns.

🏗️

Architecture Review for Cost

Evaluate architecture patterns for cost efficiency. We recommend serverless alternatives, spot instance opportunities, and container optimization strategies that reduce costs while maintaining or improving performance.

⚙️

Container & Serverless Optimization

Kubernetes resource requests and limits tuning, Lambda memory optimization, and container right-sizing with Kubecost. We ensure your containerized workloads aren't over-provisioned and your serverless functions are memory-optimized.
