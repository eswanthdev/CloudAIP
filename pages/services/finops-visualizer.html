Overview

Complete Cloud Cost Visibility

Custom cost dashboards, tagging taxonomy design, showback/chargeback reports, and anomaly detection. We build the visibility layer your organization needs to make informed decisions about cloud spending and hold teams accountable for their consumption.

What's Included

6 Visibility Deliverables

Everything you need to see, understand, and control your cloud costs.

📊

Custom Cost Dashboards

Interactive dashboards showing spend by service, team, environment, and project. Drill-down capabilities from org level to individual resource. Real-time data with historical trend analysis for informed decision-making.

🏷️

Tagging Taxonomy Design

Complete tagging strategy with mandatory and optional tags, naming conventions, and automation rules. We design a taxonomy that supports cost allocation, governance, and operational visibility across your entire cloud estate.

📄

Showback/Chargeback Reports

Automated reports showing each team and department their cloud consumption and costs. Clear, easy-to-understand breakdowns that drive accountability and enable data-driven budgeting conversations.

🚨

Anomaly Detection & Alerts

ML-based anomaly detection with configurable thresholds. Get notified before small issues become big bills. Automatic detection of unusual spending patterns, sudden cost spikes, and unexpected resource provisioning.

📈

Budget Tracking & Forecasting

Budget vs actual tracking with trend-based forecasting. Know if you'll exceed budget before it happens. Monthly, quarterly, and annual projections with confidence intervals based on historical spending patterns.

☁️

Multi-Cloud Cost Views

Unified cost views across AWS, Azure, and GCP in a single dashboard. Normalize costs across providers, compare unit economics, and get a holistic view of your total cloud investment regardless of provider.

Our Process

4 Steps to Full Visibility

From requirements to live dashboards in 3-4 weeks.

1

Requirements Workshop

We meet with stakeholders to understand your organizational structure, reporting needs, and cost allocation requirements. We define the dashboards, metrics, and alert thresholds that matter most.

2

Tagging Implementation

We design and deploy your tagging taxonomy with automation rules. Existing resources get retroactively tagged, and policy-as-code ensures new resources comply from day one.

3

Dashboard Build

We build and configure your custom dashboards, reports, anomaly detection rules, and budget tracking. Every view is tailored to its audience -- executives, engineering leads, and finance teams each get what they need.

4

Training & Handoff

Your team gets hands-on training to use, customize, and extend the dashboards independently. We provide documentation and 30 days of post-delivery support for questions and adjustments.
