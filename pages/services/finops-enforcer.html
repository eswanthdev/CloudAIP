FinOps Enforcer

Automated governance. Permanent cost control.

Overview

Governance That Never Sleeps

Automated guardrails, governance policies, tagging enforcement, and IaC cost estimation integrated into your CI/CD pipeline. Stop relying on manual reviews and tribal knowledge to control cloud costs. The FinOps Enforcer embeds cost governance directly into your engineering workflows so compliance is automatic, not aspirational.

Deliverables

What's Included

Six governance capabilities that lock in cost discipline across your entire cloud estate.

🏷️

Automated Tagging Enforcement

Policy-as-code rules that prevent resource creation without required tags. Auto-remediation for non-compliant resources ensures 100% tag coverage across your estate.

🚨

Budget Alerts & Hard Limits

Multi-level budget alerts (50%, 75%, 90%, 100%) with optional hard limits that prevent overspend. Per-team and per-project budgets give granular cost control.

📜

Policy-as-Code Implementation

AWS Service Control Policies, Azure Policies, GCP Organization Policies. Restrict expensive instance types, regions, and services before they ever get provisioned.

💰

IaC Cost Estimation in CI/CD

Integrate Infracost or custom solutions into Terraform/CloudFormation pipelines. Engineers see cost impact before deploying, making cost a first-class engineering concern.

✅

Approval Workflows for Expensive Resources

Automated approval workflows for resources exceeding cost thresholds. Slack/Teams notifications for review ensure expensive resources get human oversight before deployment.

📊

Compliance & Cost Reporting

Automated weekly/monthly compliance reports. Track tag compliance, budget adherence, and policy violations with executive-ready dashboards and trend analysis.

Technology

Tools We Use

Industry-standard tools and platforms to build governance that scales.

Terraform CloudFormation Open Policy Agent (OPA) Infracost AWS Config Rules Azure Policy Custom Lambda Functions
Process

How It Works

A structured, four-step engagement that takes you from policy design to continuous enforcement.

1

Policy Design Workshop

We work with your team to define tagging standards, budget thresholds, and governance policies aligned to your business goals.

2

Implementation & Testing

Policies are coded, tested in sandbox environments, and validated against your existing infrastructure before going live.

3

CI/CD Integration

Cost estimation and policy checks are woven into your deployment pipeline so governance is enforced automatically on every push.

4

Monitoring & Iteration

Ongoing monitoring of compliance metrics with regular policy refinement to adapt as your cloud environment evolves.
