FinOps Maturity Assessment

Understand where you are. Plan where you're going.

Overview

A Complete Cloud Financial Health Check

A one-time cloud financial management maturity evaluation that gives you a clear picture of your current FinOps capabilities and a prioritized 90-day optimization roadmap. We assess your organization across all six FinOps domains and deliver actionable recommendations with estimated savings for each.

What's Included

6 Comprehensive Deliverables

Every assessment covers the full spectrum of cloud financial management.

📊

Current State Analysis Across All 6 FinOps Domains

Comprehensive evaluation across Inform, Optimize, Operate, Organizational Alignment, Stakeholder Engagement, and Tooling. We benchmark your maturity level in each domain against industry standards.

💰

Cloud Billing & Cost Allocation Review

Complete analysis of your billing structure, account hierarchy, and cost allocation tags. We identify gaps in cost attribution and recommend improvements to ensure every dollar is accounted for.

🏷️

Tagging & Resource Organization Audit

Review of existing tags, resource groups, and organizational structure. We assess tagging compliance, identify untagged resources, and design an improved taxonomy for cost attribution and governance.

📈

Reserved Instance & Savings Plan Evaluation

Analysis of current commitments, utilization rates, and optimization opportunities. We identify unused reservations, recommend new purchases, and calculate potential savings from commitment-based discounts.

🛡️

Governance & Policy Assessment

Review of existing policies, access controls, budget alerts, and approval workflows. We evaluate your governance maturity and recommend guardrails to prevent cost overruns before they happen.

🗺️

90-Day Optimization Roadmap

Actionable, prioritized recommendations with estimated savings for each initiative. Every action is ranked by effort, impact, and risk so your team knows exactly what to tackle first for maximum ROI.

How It Works

Simple 4-Step Process

From first call to actionable roadmap in 2-3 weeks.

1

Discovery Call (30 min)

We learn about your cloud environment, business goals, and pain points. This helps us tailor the assessment to your specific situation and priorities.

2

Data Collection (3-5 days)

Read-only access to cloud billing APIs. We analyze 3-6 months of cost data to identify trends, anomalies, and optimization opportunities across your infrastructure.

3

Deep Analysis (5-7 days)

Our team evaluates every cost optimization lever across your infrastructure. We benchmark against best practices and quantify savings opportunities for each recommendation.

4

Roadmap Delivery

Detailed presentation with findings, savings estimates, and a 90-day action plan. We walk your team through every recommendation and answer all questions live.
