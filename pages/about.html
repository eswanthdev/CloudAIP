AWS

E-Commerce Platform Cost Optimization

38%
Monthly cost reduction achieved
Challenge
A mid-size e-commerce company was spending $45,000 per month on AWS with no visibility into cost drivers. Their infrastructure had grown organically over three years with over-provisioned EC2 instances, unused EBS volumes, and no reserved instance strategy in place.
Approach
Conducted a full FinOps maturity assessment and cost audit. Identified 40+ over-provisioned instances, 120 orphaned EBS volumes, and significant savings opportunities through Reserved Instances and Savings Plans. Implemented right-sizing recommendations and purchased 1-year convertible RIs for steady-state workloads.
Results
Reduced monthly AWS spend from $45,000 to $28,000 -- a 38% reduction. Implemented tagging strategy and cost allocation dashboards for ongoing visibility. Set up automated alerts and governance policies to prevent cost drift.

Azure

SaaS Startup Azure Cost Governance

$180K
Annual savings through governance and cleanup
Challenge
A fast-growing SaaS startup with 80 engineers was burning through their Azure budget with no governance framework. Developers spun up resources freely across 12 subscriptions, leading to massive sprawl. Dev/test environments ran 24/7, and there were hundreds of untagged resources with no ownership tracking.
Approach
Implemented comprehensive Azure governance using Azure Policy, Management Groups, and Cost Management. Designed a tagging taxonomy and enforced it via policy-as-code. Built automated cleanup scripts for dev/test environments using Azure Automation. Created showback reports for each engineering team and established budget alerts at the subscription level.
Results
Achieved $180,000 in annual savings through automated dev/test shutdowns, orphaned resource cleanup, and reserved instance purchases. Reduced untagged resources from 65% to under 5%. Engineering teams gained full cost visibility through team-level dashboards and weekly spend reports.

Multi-Cloud

Healthcare FinOps Practice Implementation

$320K
Annual savings across multi-cloud environment
Challenge
A healthcare technology platform operating across AWS and GCP had no centralized cloud cost management. Their combined monthly spend exceeded $80,000 with costs growing 15% quarter over quarter. Compliance requirements added complexity, and there was no FinOps function within the organization to manage optimization efforts.
Approach
Established a full FinOps practice from scratch. Built a centralized cost management platform aggregating data from both AWS and GCP. Implemented committed use discounts on GCP and Savings Plans on AWS. Optimized Kubernetes workloads using Kubecost and right-sized database instances. Created executive reporting cadence with monthly FinOps reviews and quarterly business reviews.
Results
Delivered $320,000 in annualized savings across both cloud providers. Reversed the cost growth trend, reducing quarter-over-quarter growth from 15% to 3%. Built internal FinOps capability with trained team members. Established governance and automation to maintain optimization long-term.
