FAQ

Pricing Questions

Common questions about our pricing, payment, and engagement terms.

What determines the price within each service's range? +
The price depends on the complexity of your cloud environment: number of cloud accounts/subscriptions, monthly cloud spend, number of services in use, and whether you are single-cloud or multi-cloud. During our free discovery call, we will assess your environment and provide a fixed-price quote before any engagement begins.
What are the payment terms? +
For project-based engagements (Starter, Accelerator, Complete), we require 50% upfront and 50% upon delivery. For monthly retainer services (Smart Saver, FaaS, Enterprise), payment is due at the beginning of each month. We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and can issue invoices with Net-30 terms for established clients.
Do you offer a percentage-of-savings pricing model? +
Yes, for the FinOps Optimizer service and Enterprise package, we can structure pricing as a percentage of verified savings (typically 20-30% of the savings we deliver in the first year). This model aligns our incentives with your goals and ensures we are focused on delivering maximum value. Contact us to discuss this option.
Can I upgrade my package later? +
Absolutely. Many clients start with the Starter package and upgrade to Accelerator or Complete after seeing initial results. When upgrading, you only pay the difference between packages, with a small adjustment for the bundled discount. There is no penalty for upgrading mid-engagement.
Is there a minimum contract length for retainer services? +
Smart Saver requires a minimum 3-month commitment, and FaaS (FinOps as a Service) requires a minimum 6-month commitment. This ensures enough time to implement meaningful optimizations and demonstrate ROI. After the initial commitment, services continue month-to-month with 30 days notice for cancellation.
