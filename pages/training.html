FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our training programs, tiers, and policies.

What is the difference between Ignite and Transform tiers? +
The Ignite tier focuses on core training — you get live instructor-led sessions, complete training materials, video recordings with lifetime access, hands-on labs, and exam preparation. The Transform tier includes everything in Ignite plus full career support: mock interviews with industry experts, professional resume preparation, LinkedIn profile optimization, and dedicated support until you are placed in a role. Choose Ignite if you want to upskill, choose Transform if you want a career change.
What is the training format? Are classes live or recorded? +
All courses are delivered as live, instructor-led sessions via Zoom. Classes are held on weekends (Saturday and Sunday) for 3-4 hours per session, making it convenient for working professionals. All sessions are recorded and shared within 24 hours, so you can review them anytime. You get lifetime access to the recorded content and course materials in both Ignite and Transform tiers.
How does the placement support work in the Transform tier? +
The Transform tier includes end-to-end career support. After completing your training, our career team will help you build a professional resume tailored to cloud roles, optimize your LinkedIn profile for recruiter visibility, and conduct multiple rounds of mock interviews simulating real hiring processes. We continue to provide active support — including job referrals, application guidance, and interview coaching — until you land a role. There is no time limit on this support.
What are the prerequisites for enrolling? +
For the FinOps Training, basic cloud knowledge is recommended but not required — we cover the fundamentals before diving deeper. For the AWS AI Training, we recommend having basic familiarity with AWS services or general programming concepts. Both programs are designed to take you from foundational knowledge to certification-ready, regardless of your starting point.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer a full refund if you cancel within 7 days of enrollment and before the second class session. After the second session, we offer a 50% refund or the option to defer to the next batch at no additional cost. If you are not satisfied with the training quality after the first session, we will issue a complete refund — no questions asked.
Can I upgrade from Ignite to Transform later? +
Yes, you can upgrade from Ignite to Transform at any point during or after your training. You will only pay the difference between the two tiers. All your existing progress, recordings, and materials carry over seamlessly. Many students start with Ignite and upgrade to Transform once they decide they want full career support.
